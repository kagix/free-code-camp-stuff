Albert Schweitzer

The man who reinvented the pipe organ

  • 1875 - Born in Kayserberg.
  • 1885 - Started to study organ in Mulhouse for the next 8 years.
  • 1899 - Identified figures and mothifs in Bach's Chorale Preludes.
  • 1905 - Published J. S. Bach: Le Musicien-Poète.
  • 1906 - Published The Art of Organ Building
  • 1913 - Departure for Lambaréné.
  • 1952 - Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 1965 - Dies at 90.
